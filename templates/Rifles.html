Rifles

Rifles are long-barreled firearms designed for precision shooting. They are typically fired from the shoulder and are popular for hunting, sport shooting, and military use. Rifles are often classified into different types:
Assault Rifles: Selective-fire rifles designed for military applications. Examples include AK-47, M16, and FN SCAR.
Sniper Rifles: High-precision rifles designed for long-range shooting. Examples include Barrett M82, Remington 700, and Accuracy International AXMC.
Rifles are valued for their range, accuracy, and effectiveness in various situations.


AK-47

A widely recognized assault rifle known for its durability and effectiveness in combat.

M16

An iconic military rifle, known for its accuracy and fire rate, used by the U.S. Army.

FN SCAR

A modular assault rifle known for its versatility and performance in various environments.

Barrett M82

A heavy sniper rifle capable of firing long-range precision shots with high-powered ammunition.

Remington 700

A bolt-action rifle known for its accuracy, often used by hunters and military snipers.

Accuracy International AXMC

A high-precision sniper rifle with modular features, used by elite military units.
