Sniper Rifles

Sniper Rifles are precision firearms designed for long-range engagements. They are widely used by military forces, law enforcement, and sport shooters for their accuracy and power. They are typically categorized into two main types:
Bolt-Action Rifles: Known for their reliability and precision. Examples include Remington 700, Accuracy International AX50, and Winchester Model 70.
Semi-Automatic Rifles: Provide faster follow-up shots without compromising accuracy. Examples include Barrett M82, Dragunov SVD, and HK G28.
Sniper Rifles are valued for their ability to neutralize targets at extreme distances with high precision.
