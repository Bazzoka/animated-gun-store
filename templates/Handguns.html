Handguns

Handguns are small, portable firearms designed to be held and operated with one hand. They are widely used for self-defense, law enforcement, and sport shooting. They are typically categorized into two main types:
Pistols: Semi-automatic firearms that use magazines to load multiple rounds. Examples include Glock 19, Beretta 92FS, and SIG Sauer P320.
Revolvers: Firearms with a rotating cylinder that holds multiple rounds. Examples include Colt Python, Smith & Wesson Model 29, and Ruger GP100.
Handguns are valued for their compact size, ease of concealment, and versatility in various scenarios.
