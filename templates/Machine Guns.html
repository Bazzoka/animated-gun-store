Machine Guns

Machine guns are fully automatic firearms capable of sustained rapid fire. They are primarily used in military operations for providing suppressive fire. They are typically categorized into two main types:
Light Machine Guns (LMGs): Portable automatic firearms designed for infantry use. Examples include M249 SAW, RPK, and FN Minimi.
Heavy Machine Guns (HMGs): Larger-caliber automatic firearms designed for longer-range and sustained fire. Examples include M2 Browning, NSV, and DShK.
Machine guns are valued for their high rate of fire and ability to dominate combat zones with suppressive firepower.
